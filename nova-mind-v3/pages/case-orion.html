The Challenge

Orion Group, a mid-size retail distributor with 8 regional warehouses, was losing an estimated €2.4M per year to overstock and stockout events. Their planning team relied on spreadsheet-based forecasting, which couldn't account for seasonal trends, regional demand variance, or supplier lead time variability.

They needed a system that could forecast demand at the SKU-location level across a 90-day horizon — and integrate with their existing ERP without a full migration.

Our Approach

Nova Mind ran a 2-week discovery sprint to map data sources, identify forecast-relevant signals, and define success metrics. We then built a three-stage solution:

1. Data foundation: Unified 4 years of sales data, supplier records, and regional marketing spend into a clean feature store using Apache Airflow and dbt.

2. Forecasting engine: Trained an ensemble model combining LightGBM for baseline demand and a custom Temporal Fusion Transformer for multi-horizon prediction. Achieved MAPE of 8.3% across all SKU-location pairs.

3. Planner dashboard: Built a React-based planning UI showing forecast confidence intervals, anomaly alerts, and one-click reorder recommendations. Integrated via REST API with their SAP ERP.

Outcome

Deployed to production in week 14. In the first full quarter, Orion reduced overstock costs by 34% — equivalent to €820,000 in annualized savings. The planning team adopted the tool fully within the first month, with zero training sessions required due to the intuitive interface.

Nova Mind continues to support the platform on a retainer basis, adding new product categories and expanding the model to two new distribution regions.
